Jenna deBoisblanc (b. 1989) is a net artist, creative technologist, and former computer science teacher from New Orleans. She received her undergraduate degree in physics from Pomona College and her MFA in digital art from Tulane University. Her work has been shown at The Front, Good Children Gallery, the Contemporary Arts Center New Orleans, Luna Fête, and on digital platforms including The Wrong Biennale and SIGGRAPH. She has also worked with clients including the Florida Aquarium, Marriott, and Toyota.

She currently serves as Director of Data Dashboards at New Orleans Public Schools, where she builds and maintains the NOLA-PS Data Dashboard .

Jenna is also the founder and curator of Public Access Memories, a virtual net art gallery that situates digital artworks in their natural online environment.
