About

Jenna deBoisblanc is a creative coder (and former computer science teacher) from New Orleans, LA. She received her undergraduate degree in physics from Pomona College (Claremont, CA) and her MFA in digital arts from Tulane University (New Orleans, LA). She has shown work at the New Orleans Contemporary Art Center; galleries including The Front, Good Children, and Santa Barbara Community Arts Center; at light festivals in the South (Luna Fête, Light Up Albuquerque); and on digital platforms including The Wrong Biennale and SIGGRAPH. Her clients include Toyota, Marriott International, the Florida Aquarium, and NASA.
